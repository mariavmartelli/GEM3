GEM3

Geophysical Modeling and Multiscale Methods

About

The GEM3 project aims at developing, analyzing and performing parallel and machine learning implementations of innovative multiscale finite element methods for linear and nonlinear elliptic and parabolic partial differential equations, arising from applications in surface and subsurface geophysics. This three-year international collaboration involves the research project-team G from Inria/France and the research project-team IPES from LNCC/Brazil.
